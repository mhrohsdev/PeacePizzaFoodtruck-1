Our Goal

An Unforgettable Experience

When you book Peace Pizza, you get more than just great food. We bring the full setup -- our mobile wood-fired oven, all the fresh ingredients, and a team that loves what they do. Your guests get to watch every pizza crafted from scratch and pulled from the flames in under 90 seconds.

It's not just catering -- it's an interactive experience that becomes the highlight of your event. Everyone gathers around the oven, picks their toppings, and enjoys something truly special together.

  • Wood-Fired Pizzas - $10, any toppings same price
  • Garlic Twists - Fresh baked, crowd favorite
  • Blueberry Summer Seed Salad - Poppyseed dressing & more
