Our Story

The passion behind every pizza

From The Owner

A Passion for Pizza & People

Hey there -- I'm Max, and I want to tell you a little bit about how Peace Pizza came to be. It all started with a simple love for great food and bringing people together. I've always believed that there's something special about gathering around a fire and sharing a meal, and wood-fired pizza just happens to be the perfect way to do that.

When I first got into wood-fired cooking, I was hooked immediately. The way the flames kiss the dough, the smoky aroma that fills the air, the look on someone's face when they take that first bite of a pizza pulled straight from a real wood-fired oven -- there's nothing else like it. I knew I had to share that experience with as many people as I could. That's how Peace Pizza was born: out of a genuine love for the craft and a desire to bring something truly special to the Cincinnati community.

What drives me every single day is the people. Whether we're pulling up to a backyard graduation party, a wedding reception, or a big corporate event, every single one is personal to me. I take the time to make sure the dough is perfect, the ingredients are fresh, and the experience is something your guests will remember. This isn't just a job -- it's my passion, and I pour my heart into every pizza we make.

Thank you for considering Peace Pizza for your next event. I can't wait to fire up the oven, stretch some dough, and create something delicious for you and your guests. Let's make it an event to remember.

— Max, Owner & Pizzaiolo
