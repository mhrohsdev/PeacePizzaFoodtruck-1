Our Dough

Handmade with Authentic Italian Flavor

Every great pizza starts with great dough. Our dough is handmade fresh daily using an authentic Italian recipe—premium flour, pure water, natural yeast, and sea salt. Each batch is mixed, kneaded by hand, and given time to develop real flavor and perfect texture.

Every dough ball is hand-stretched, rested for a slow rise, then cooked at high heat in our wood-fired oven in under 90 seconds. Crispy, lightly charred exterior with a soft, chewy interior—that unmistakable smoky flavor only a real wood-fired oven delivers.
